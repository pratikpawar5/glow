Why list on gloqr?

gloqr®

the business social app
Like human beings businesses also need social environment to grow fast and effectively. gloqr is a digital platform specially designed for Small and Medium Enterprises (SME’s). gloqr offers following features for SME’s :

gloqr shop

List your business on gloqr precisely with detail information to go global. You can list your business information like, 1) Products you manufacture or deal with. 2) Services you offer. 3) Infrastructure like machinery, building or instruments. 4) Display work culture in galleries. 5) Show the powerful minds behind the business in management team (section) with experience and expertise

gloqr jobs

Recruit the operators and office staff with gloqr. Just upload the job recquirement and start receiving resumes of interested candidates.

gloqr salesman

Now send quotations without human interference, let gloqr be your sales executive. Send instant quotations when you are not in office or enjoying your holidays. Manage all the leads at one place in cart.

gloqr mylead

Allows you to receive a lead information clicked on your products or services. gloqr never shares your leads to other suppliers, your lead is only yours. Other lead generating sites share your product or services lead to multiple suppliers which lowers the possibility of conversion of lead to business

gloqr circle

Don’t sit idle in wait and watch mode, Now take the proactive approach. Connect with other businesses or prospective customers by sending Circle Invite Request & build your Business Social Circle. You can broadcast specific business information to your business circle like 1) Purchase of a new machinery. 2) Start of a new branch. 3) Acquisition of new big a customer. 4) Completion of a flagship project. 5) Successful delivery and installation of a critical project. 6) New product release, new capacity addition and so on. Let your Business circle know what you are doing, and if you want to share the same information openly to all the people globally then post it in public mode, That information will appear on your gloqr page in social post section.
